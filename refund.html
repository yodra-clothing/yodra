Refund Policy

At YODRA, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You may request a refund within 7 days of receiving your order, provided the product is unused, unworn, and in its original packaging.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • Items purchased on sale or with a discount code
  • Used or damaged products
  • Items without original packaging

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at info@yodra.in with your order number and reason for return. Once approved, we’ll share the return shipping details.

4. Refund Process

After we receive and inspect your return, we’ll notify you of the approval. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Shipping Costs

Return shipping charges are the customer’s responsibility unless the item is defective or incorrect.
