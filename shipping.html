Shipping & Delivery

We’re committed to delivering your orders quickly and efficiently across India.

1. Shipping Regions

We currently ship across all major cities and towns in India. International shipping will be available soon.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after receiving the order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

3. Delivery Time

  • Standard Shipping: 7-14 business days
  • Express Shipping: 7 business days
  • Rural or remote areas may take additional time.

4. Shipping Charges

We offer free standard shipping on orders over ₹999. Express shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

5. Tracking Your Order

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped. You can use it to track your package on our courier partner's website.

6. Delivery Issues

If your order is delayed or missing, please contact us at info@yodra.in and we’ll help you resolve it quickly.
